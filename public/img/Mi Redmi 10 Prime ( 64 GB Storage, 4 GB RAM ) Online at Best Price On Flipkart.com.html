    Product Description
    Powerful Battery

    Thanks to its 6000 mAh battery, the Redmi 10 Prime Smartphone offers a long battery life. So, you can juice it up and use it throughout the day without interrupting your work or entertainment. And, with 18 W fast charging, it quickly gets charged up so that you won’t have to keep it on charge for hours.

    Storage and Speed

    With up to 6 GB of RAM and up to 128 GB of internal storage, this smartphone lets you enjoy a smooth user experience while also storing your videos, pictures, files, and much more. So, you can carry all your data along wherever you go.

    MediaTek Helio G88

    Featuring Intelligent Multi-network Prediction Engine 2.0, this smartphone allows interruption-free gaming performance even when the network is congested. Also, with Rapid Response Engine 2.0, it ensures low latency and quick touch response for smooth gaming. Moreover, with Resource Management Engine 2.0, this smartphone can balance GPU, CUP, and other such processing elements. And, its refresh rate of up to 90 Hz adds ample speed and break-free gaming at all times.

    Dual Mic and Speakers

    Featuring up to two mics, this smartphone allows callers to hear your voice clearly. And, its dual speakers let you enjoy loud and clear music for entertainment. So, you can even enjoy listening to music without having to connect to speakers while you’re watching movies in your room.

    Camera Features

    With an 8 MP in-display selfie camera, this smartphone allows clear shots and panorama selfies as well that you would love. Also, its 2 MP macro camera captures close-up and detailed pictures for you. And, its 2 MP depth sensor helps with the correct focal length and background blurring. Moreover, its 50 MP primary camera and 8 MP ultra-wide camera help with daily photography.
